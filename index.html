About Us

About RKLM School

RKLM Girls Senior Secondary School is a CBSE-affiliated girls school established in 1949 under the Ramjas Foundation. Located at 2107/37 Desh Bandhu Gupta Road, Karol Bagh, New Delhi, it provides education from Grade VI to XII and focuses on academic excellence, values, leadership and holistic development.

Message from the Principal

Welcome to RKLM School. As a CBSE-affiliated girls senior secondary school under the Ramjas Foundation legacy, we are committed to helping every student from Grade VI to XII grow with confidence, discipline, values and academic strength.

Together with our dedicated teachers and supportive parents, we work to create a learning environment where girls are encouraged to lead, serve, excel and become responsible citizens.
